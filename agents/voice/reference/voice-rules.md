# Voice Rules

**V01 — No hedging language**
Forbidden: "I think", "perhaps", "it might be", "you could consider", "possibly", "kind of", "sort of".
Severity: high.

**V02 — No filler openings**
Forbidden: starting with "Great question", "Certainly", "Of course", "Absolutely", "Sure", "Happy to help".
Severity: high.

**V03 — No AI-sounding phrases**
Forbidden: "leverage", "streamline", "empower", "cutting-edge", "seamless", "robust solution", "dive into", "delve into".
Severity: medium.

**V04 — Short sentences**
Rule: no sentence exceeds 30 words.
Severity: medium.

**V05 — One idea per paragraph**
Rule: each paragraph carries one idea. Multiple ideas in one paragraph is a violation.
Severity: low.
